Daily Updates for BC!

by BC Support - March 12, 2020

It has been nearly a year of Bread Clicker. In the first few months, there were nearly daily updates to the game, but around May we slowed down on the rate of the updates to zero. In late fall of 2019, our team brought back the game and tried to make Bread Clicker a much better game. Since October, we have brought dozens of new concepts and additions to the game that made our game so much more enjoyable to play. However, February saw no updates. We plan to make March, the birth month of this game, as active as we can with updates. Get excited for the future of Bread Clicker!
